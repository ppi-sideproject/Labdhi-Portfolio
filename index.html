About

The Purple Ribbon Project was started by ARMY(BTS Fandom) in 2018. Fans used purple ribbons and made a human chain at the airport so that the members could move easily without any hassle. The initiative once again showed how much fans care for the group. Following the project, V was so touched by it that he even posed with a purple ribbon during a photoshoot for Dispatch.

Airport Protection

The original action in 2018 to ensure a safe passage for the members.

A Member's Heart

The heartfelt recognition of the project by V, making the color official.

Global ARMY Unity

The project represents the worldwide, protective spirit of the fandom.
